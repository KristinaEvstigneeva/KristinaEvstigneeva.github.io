About the cultural event

Russia-Italy, Italy-Russia: Through the Centuries From Giotto to Malevich

It is a remarkable exhibition that explores the rich and dynamic cultural exchanges between Russia and Italy over several centuries. This exhibition showcases masterpieces from renowned artists, tracing the artistic dialogues and influences from the Italian Renaissance to the Russian Avant-Garde.

Visitors will journey through time, witnessing the evolution of artistic styles and the profound impact these two nations have had on each other’s cultural landscapes. Featuring works from Giotto, a master of the early Italian Renaissance, to Kazimir Malevich, a pioneer of abstract art in Russia, this exhibition highlights the continuous and reciprocal artistic inspiration that has shaped the art world.

Through paintings, sculptures, and multimedia installations, the exhibition offers a unique perspective on the interconnected histories and artistic achievements of these two great cultures.
